Administrative updates

 

Crisis Communication Plan

 

It is imperative that Pluribus Digital identifies procedures that will enable us to respond quickly to an emergency event and ensure continuous performance of critical business functions. As part of the procedure, it is vital that your manager has your contact information (phone, email) to make you aware of any emergency situations. Please let us know via the HelpDesk if you have any concerns with your manager having this information.

 

HelpDesk Usage

 

Reminding everyone to please send questions and requests to our Helpdesk instead of reaching out directly to one person via slack or direct email. This allows us to track the types of requests and, more importantly, gets you a faster response. Tom, Halai and Kim all have access to all HelpDesk inquiries, giving us cover for each other when any of us are out-of-office. Sample requests to the HelpDesk include, but are not limited to:

 
  • Timekeeping - i.e. - to reopen a timesheet, request charge codes, report problems,
  • benefits - changes or questions,
  • payroll questions,
  • training requests,
  • expense reimbursement,
  • report spam, etc

Suspicious messages

 

Stay vigilant for email, text and even calls that are suspect. Report anything you think is out of the ordinary to the HelpDesk. Review your daily email quarantine log for flagged emails that are safe, there is a “Release” link for each quarantined email.

If you are interested in certification in either, or both, please let us know. If you have any existing certs, you can add those to the Pluribus family as well. They are yours and go where you go! Let us know at the HelpDesk.
